1. Introduction

Welcome to Easy English (hereinafter referred to as "the Application"). This policy explains how we handle your personal information to ensure your data security and privacy while using the app.

We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our features:

・Personally Identifiable Information:
To display personalized content in the app, we collect the "Name or Nickname" that you voluntarily enter. You are free to choose whether to use your real name or a nickname.
・Device and Technical Information:
To maintain system operations, we automatically collect the following items:
・Device Identifier: Such as Android ID (Android) or IDFV (iOS).

We use the device identifier to determine whether it is the same device, helping us distinguish between new and returning users and ensuring your settings and progress are correctly linked.

To help us provide, maintain, and continuously improve the compatibility and functional experience of the app.

This application integrates Google AdMob.

・Trigger Mechanism:
Ads will only be loaded and displayed when you actively click or agree to watch them.

AdMob may collect your advertising ID for frequency capping or anonymous analysis.

5. Account and Data Deletion (Important Notice)

We give you full control over your personal data:

・Deletion Path:
You can delete your data at any time via the top-right corner in the app: "Settings" -> "Delete Account".
・Consequences:
Please note that once you confirm the deletion of your account, the system will still recognize that this device has a history of use even if you reinstall the application in the future. As a result, you will not be eligible to receive exclusive benefits or offers reserved for "new users" again.

6. Policy Updates and Announcements

When there are updates to this privacy policy or the app version, we will notify you through the app's internal "Announcements" feature.
