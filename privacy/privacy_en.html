Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Easy English Learning (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This policy outlines how we handle your personal information to ensure data security and privacy during your use of our services.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our features:

・Personally Identifiable Information:
To personalize your experience within the App, we collect the "Name or Nickname" that you provide. You may choose to use either your real name or a pseudonym.
・Device and Technical Information:
To maintain system operations, we automatically collect the following:
・Device Identifiers: e.g., Android ID (Android) or IDFV (iOS).
・System Settings: System language, platform type, App version, and screen size.

3. Purpose of Data Usage

・User Identification:
We use device identifiers to recognize returning users, ensuring your settings and progress are correctly linked.
・Service Optimization:
To help us provide, maintain, and continuously improve the compatibility and functional experience of the App.

4. Third-Party Services: AdMob Advertising

The App integrates Google AdMob.

・Trigger Mechanism:
Ads are only loaded and displayed when you actively click on or agree to watch them.
・Data Processing:
AdMob may collect your Advertising ID for frequency capping or anonymous analytics.

5. Account and Data Deletion (Important Notice)

We provide you with full control over your personal data:

・Deletion Path:
You can perform a deletion at any time via "Settings" -> "Delete Account" in the top-right corner of the App.
・Consequences:
Please note that once you confirm the account deletion, the system will still recognize that the device has a prior record. Therefore, you will not be eligible for "New User" benefits or promotions upon reinstallation.

6. Policy Updates and Announcements

When there are updates to this Privacy Policy or the App version, we will notify you through the "App Announcements" feature.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please reach out through the following channels:

・In-App Feature:
Via the "Contact Us" function within the App.
・Email:
Go to "Settings" -> "About Developer Team" to find our contact information.
