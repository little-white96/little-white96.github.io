1. Introduction

Welcome to eLearning Easy English Learning (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This policy outlines how we handle your personal information to ensure data security and privacy during your use of our services.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our features:

・Personally Identifiable Information:
To personalize your experience in the App, we collect the "Name or Nickname" you actively provide. You may choose to use either your real name or a pseudonym.
・Device and Technical Information:
To maintain system operations, we automatically collect the following:
・Device Identifiers: Such as Android ID (Android) or IDFV (iOS).
・System Settings: Language preferences, platform type, App version, and screen size.

3. Purpose of Data Usage

・User Identification:
We use device identifiers to verify the same device, distinguish between new and returning users, and ensure your settings and progress are correctly linked.
・Service Optimization:
To help us provide, maintain, and continuously improve the compatibility and functional experience of the App.

4. Third-Party Services: AdMob Ads

The App integrates Google AdMob.

・Trigger Mechanism:
Ads are loaded and displayed only when you actively click or consent to view them.

AdMob may collect your Advertising ID for frequency capping or anonymous analytics.

5. Account and Data Deletion (Important)

We grant you full control over your personal data:

・Deletion Path:
You can perform a deletion at any time via "Settings" -> "Delete Account" in the top-right corner of the App.
・Consequences:
Please note that once you confirm the account deletion, even if you reinstall the App in the future, the system will still recognize the device's previous usage history. Consequently, you will not be eligible for "New User" benefits or promotions again.

6. Policy Updates and Announcements

When there are updates to this Privacy Policy or the App version, we will notify you through the "Announcements" feature within the App.
